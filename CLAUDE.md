## BMAD Agents Reference

### Core Development Team

**analyst** - Market Research & Analysis
- Market research and competitive analysis
- Requirements gathering and stakeholder interviews
- Project briefs and market opportunity assessment
- User research and persona development
- Business case development

**pm** - Product Management
- Creates Product Requirements Documents (PRDs)
- Defines features and product specifications
- Prioritizes product roadmaps and backlogs
- Stakeholder communication and alignment
- Product strategy and vision development

**architect** - System Architecture
- Designs system architecture and technical blueprints
- Makes technical decisions and technology choices
- Plans for scalability and performance optimization
- Database design and API specifications
- Infrastructure and deployment architecture

**dev** - Development & Implementation
- Writes code and implements features
- Debugging and troubleshooting issues
- Follows technical specifications and coding standards
- Code reviews and optimization
- Integration and deployment tasks

**qa** - Quality Assurance
- Tests everything thoroughly (functional, integration, performance)
- Finds bugs and creates detailed bug reports
- Ensures quality standards and best practices
- Test planning and test case development
- Automated testing implementation

**ux-expert** - User Experience Design
- Designs user interfaces and interaction patterns
- Creates user flows and wireframes
- Develops prototypes and design specifications
- UI/UX research and usability testing
- Design system development

**po** - Product Owner
- Manages product backlog and priorities
- Validates user stories and acceptance criteria
- Ensures project consistency and requirements alignment
- Stakeholder communication and feedback collection
- Sprint planning and release coordination

**sm** - Scrum Master/Project Management
- Creates and manages user stories
- Manages sprints and agile ceremonies
- Tracks progress and removes blockers
- Team coordination and process improvement
- Metrics tracking and reporting

### Quick Selection Guide
- **Research & Planning**: analyst, pm
- **Technical Design**: architect
- **Implementation**: dev
- **Quality & Testing**: qa
- **User Experience**: ux-expert
- **Project Management**: po, sm
